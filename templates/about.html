What was your goal with this project?

  • My goal was to create a functional, interactive, and super lightweight web app that allows MTG players to keep tabs on Alpha, Beta, and Unlimited Power 9 from eBay. The idea is to measure power and, eventually, other cards that move in 'herds' (are closely correlated). This was a learning project first and foremost, so please be kind ðŸ’—.
What stack did you build this with? Why?

  • Linux, Nginx, PostgreSQL, JavaScript, and Python (shout-out to Flask). Fast to implement with, easy to iterate over, and great for working with anything data-related, especially data needing to be populated by multiple unique scripts.
  • Populated with eBay's API.
  • Hosted through DigitalOcean.
How often do you currently update the data?

  • I am currently fetching the data once a day via a scheduled cron job @ 12:30pm CST 11:59PM UTC (6:59PM CST) for the time being ðŸ˜³.

What kind of range is your data based on?

  • Pretty much everything you see is based on a 90 day rolling average, with the exception being `Spot Price` and `Active Listing Count`.
